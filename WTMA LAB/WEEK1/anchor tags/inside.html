The Good Dinosaur is a 2015 Pixar animated film set in an alternate history where dinosaurs never went extinct, following a timid Apatosaurus named Arlo who gets lost and befriends a human caveboy, Spot, on a journey home, learning to overcome his fears and find his courage. The film explores themes of family, loss, fear, and self-discovery, showcasing beautiful natural landscapes contrasting with the main characters' struggles in a world where dinosaurs evolved to be intelligent and humans remained primitive.
